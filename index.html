Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common inquiries about Thila Homam, including its benefits, procedure, and booking process. Contact us for personalized guidance.

What is Thila Homam?

Thila Homam is a Vedic ritual offering sesame seeds to appease ancestors, alleviate curses, and promote family prosperity.

Who should perform Thila Homam?

Those facing ancestral issues or seeking peace for departed souls should perform Thila Homam for spiritual relief and family harmony.

Where is Thila Homam performed?

Thila Homam is traditionally performed near holy waters, such as seashores or rivers, to enhance the ritual’s spiritual impact.

How long does Thila Homam take?

The Thila Homam ceremony typically takes a few hours, depending on the ritual's complexity and the number of offerings made.

What are the benefits of Thila Homam?

Thila Homam brings peace to departed souls, alleviates ancestral curses, promotes family prosperity, and ensures spiritual upliftment and harmony.

What materials are needed for Thila Homam?

Essential materials include sesame seeds, ghee, sacred wood, and specific offerings as per Vedic guidelines, all used in the ritual fire.
